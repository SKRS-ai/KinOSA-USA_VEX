We don't just seek funding; we seek synergy. Below are the non-profit institutions and government bodies whose missions directly align with the Makinzine Pro technical mentorship ecosystem.

Target Grantor // Media Innovation

The Lenfest Institute

Reason for Alignment:

As Lenfest focuses on the sustainability of local journalism, the Makinzine Pro provides the hardware sanctuary for that journalism to be consumed without distraction. We seek to partner on their **Philadelphia Media Founders Exchange** to train our youth mentors in the distribution of high-impact news through the .kin container.
