Operational Queries

How do I verify my device's authenticity?

Every Makinzine Pro is laser-etched with a unique Vexilla ID that corresponds to a record in the Vanguard Registry. You can verify your hardware by scanning the ID within the Secure Portal.

What is the "Naked Mode" reset?

A physical hardware sequence that restores the KinOS kernel to its factory state, permanently erasing all locally cached data in the .kin container for absolute security.
